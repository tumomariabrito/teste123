🌡️ Tips on How to Not Melt

Being a snowman in a warm world takes some skill. I've learned a few things along the way — and I'm happy to share! You're welcome. 😄

  • ❄️
    Get your own personal flurry cloud. Having a magical friend like Elsa really helps. She made me a little snow cloud that follows me everywhere. If you don't have one, you might want to start making the right friends!
  • 🌲
    Stay in the shade whenever possible. Trees are very underrated. They're cool, they're tall, and they don't judge you. Find a good shady spot and hang out there on the hot days.
  • 💨
    Always be near a breeze. A cool wind can be a snowman's best friend. Position yourself near a window, a fan, or a friendly dragon who promises to blow cold air.
  • 🧊
    Carry a bucket of snow with you. This one takes a little planning, but it's worth it! If you feel warm, just apply some extra snow. It's like sunscreen — but better.
  • 😌
    Stay calm and think cool thoughts. Stress makes you warm. So take a breath, think of snowflakes, frozen tundras, and that beautiful moment when the sky turns all white and sparkly. Ahhh. Much better.
  • 🤗
    Warm hugs in moderation. I know, I know — I LOVE warm hugs. But if you're mostly made of snow, it's probably wise to keep hugs brief. One second. Two, tops. Then step back.
  • ⏰
    Visit the beach only at sunset. The golden hour is magical AND less dangerous temperature-wise. Plus you look absolutely great in that light. Trust me on this one.
